Chimain Douglas Ministries -- "Setting The People Free"

Chimain Douglas has been in ministry for over 20 years.  She is a preacher and teacher of God's Word.  God has commissioned, called, and chosen her to set His people free.  Mrs. Douglas has traveled all over to teach God's people the principles of going to another level; operating in a greater anointing, and doing greater works for the Lord Jesus Christ.  Chimain Douglas is a wife, mother, licensed minister, teacher and vessel for the Lord.

Chimain Douglas Ministries is a Christian Ministry dedicated to providing biblical knowledge to set God's people Free.  We believe that Jesus Christ is the Son of God; We believe in the Word of God; We are led by the Holy Spirit; We apply the Word of God to all situations of life; We believe that God has already given us everything we need for life and life more abundantly.  Chimain's prayer is that God will open the eyes of His people to see, their ears to hear, and their hearts and minds to operate in His power and anointing, and to do greater works for Him and His Kingdom.  

Chimain has been mandated, appointed, anointed, and ordained by God Himself to renew people's minds, to break traditions, to open eyes, and to set His people free.  Chimain is a Servant and Vessel for God.

Chimain Douglas' Bio

Chimain Douglas is a native of Mobile, Alabama, but currently resides in Atlanta, GA.  She received her Bachelor of Science Degree in Accounting and Business from Alabama State University in Montgomery, Alabama.  She worked as an Auditor with the Federal Goverment for 12 years.  Chimain is a licensed Minister through Greater Morning Star Missionary Baptist Church in Mobile, Alabama.  She attended Oral Roberts/Richard Roberts School of the Holy Spirit.  She was a member of a local church in Lithonia, Georgia over 20 years where she served in the counseling ministry for 10 years.  Chimain has received Biblical Counseling licenses from the Christian Research Development Institute, the American Association of Christian Counselors, and the Georgia Board of Examiners Christian Counselors and Therapists. Chimain founded a "A House Divided Cannot Stand Marriage Ministry" in 1991 where she held conferences, taught classes and preached to numerous pre-marital and married couples.   Chimain began Chimain Douglas Ministries, "Setting the People Free",  in 2005 in an effort to set God's people free through preaching and teaching about the Power and Anointing of the Holy Spirit, and going to another level in God.  God has used Chimain in a mighty way to open spiritual eyes and ears so that people will see and hear what God is speaking in this season.  It is time to go to another level in God and to do Greater works for Him.  God has given us His Power and Anointing to do miracles, signs and wonders for His Kingdom.


God is a Good God, and He is so Faithful.  I love Him with all my heart and I want to serve Him all my life.  In Him, I live, move and have my being.  Use me Lord for Your Glory!  Chimain Douglas
